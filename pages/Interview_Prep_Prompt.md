# Interview Preparation Prompt (English)

> A ChatGPT prompt template for structured mock interview practice.
> Source: AI Prompts Telegram Channel (@aiindi)

---

## The Prompt

```
Act as a senior interviewer for the role of [YOUR TARGET ROLE].

Here is how I want you to train me:

1️⃣ Ask me my experience level and primary skill set.

2️⃣ Start a mock interview for the role of [YOUR TARGET ROLE].
   • Ask one question at a time
   • Mix fundamentals, scenarios, and follow-ups

3️⃣ After each answer:
   • Rate my response out of 10
   • Point out gaps
   • Share a strong sample answer

4️⃣ If I answer well, go deeper.
   • Edge cases
   • Real project decisions
   • Trade-offs

5️⃣ Track my weak areas during the interview.

6️⃣ After the interview:
   • List weak topics
   • Suggest what to revise first
   • Give 5 clear improvement actions
```

---

## How It Works

| Step | What Happens | Value |
|------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Assess level & skills | Sets appropriate difficulty |
| 2 | Mock interview begins | One question at a time, mixed types |
| 3 | Feedback per answer | Score + gaps + sample answer |
| 4 | Adaptive depth | Edge cases & trade-offs for strong answers |
| 5 | Weak area tracking | Real-time monitoring |
| 6 | Post-interview report | Weak topics + revision order + 5 actions |

---

## Usage Tips

- Replace `[YOUR TARGET ROLE]` with your specific role (e.g., "Senior Backend Engineer", "Product Manager")
- Be honest about experience level — the AI adjusts difficulty accordingly
- After the interview, save the weak topic list and create a study plan
- Repeat weekly to track improvement
